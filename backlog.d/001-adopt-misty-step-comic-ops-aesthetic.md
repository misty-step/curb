# Adopt the Misty Step comic-ops aesthetic baseline

Priority: P3 · Status: pending · Estimate: M

## Goal
Evaluate whether Curb should adopt the comic-ops baseline for fleet, agent,
or operator-status surfaces.

## Oracle
- [ ] A design note names the chosen flavor, likely `operator-pulp` or
      `clean-atomic`, and the surfaces it governs.
- [ ] One representative fleet/status surface is rendered or mocked with hard
      panels, proof strips, and readable operational density.
- [ ] The design preserves operator clarity and does not turn status into
      decoration.
- [ ] The implementation uses `@misty-step/aesthetic` commit `9bbe0f9` or later,
      or records a deliberate no-adoption decision.
- [ ] The repo's canonical gate passes after implementation.
